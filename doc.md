# 🍜 How to use Lite Noodle

Lite Noodle saves your computer's memory by putting inactive tabs to sleep (unloading). Here is how it works.

## The Essentials
1. **Let it flow:** The extension monitors your tabs in the background. If you don't use a tab for a while, it is automatically put to sleep.
2. **Instant wake-up:** To reactivate a tab, simply click on it. It will reload exactly where you left off.
3. **Zero loss:** Tabs playing sound (music, video) are **never** automatically put to sleep.

## Settings (inside the extension menu)
- **Limit:** Choose the delay before a tab is put to sleep (e.g., 15 min, 1h, 30s).
- **Discard Pinned Tabs:** If checked, even your pinned tabs will be put to sleep.
- **Always reset timers on change:** If checked, every change to your settings will apply the new delay to all tabs, regardless of their current progress.
- **"Discard Others" button:** Click here to manually put all inactive tabs to sleep at once.

## Customization
- Use the toggle in the top right corner to switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode.

---
*Good to know: You can see the time remaining before each tab is put to sleep directly within the extension's list.*
